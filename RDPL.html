Romeo Distric Library Logo



This project was to create a new logo for the Romeo District Library.







The logo was designed with assisstance from heads at the library to flesh out idea. It came from the idea that books are the foundation of growing, and included trees growing out of it to connect it to the culture of the city. The logo was ultimately never used, but ended with a strong final outcome.
